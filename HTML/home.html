Who am I

My name is Yelena Baranchuk. I am Quality Assurance Engineer with 4 years of experience in manual, mobile and automation testing. Right now I am full time employee in Amdocs company and a part time student at Edmonds Community College. I take classes as part of Web Applications and Cloud Developer degree.

In my free time from school and work I like traveling, hiking, camping, walking with my dog as well as spending time with my friends. I have the most adorable dog. She is one year old Golden Retriever named Skye.
